## Create Your Account

Set up your account in minutes to access personalized feeds and creator tools.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the App" icon="download">
    Install TikTok or CapCut from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Open the app and tap **Profile** > **Sign up**. Use your email, phone, or social login like Google or Apple.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify" icon="check-circle">
    Enter the verification code sent to your email or phone. Complete your profile with a username and photo.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use a strong password and enable two-factor authentication for security.
</Callout>

## Log In and Navigate

Access your account across platforms with seamless navigation.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Launch the app, tap **Profile** in the bottom right, then **Log in**. Swipe through **For You**, **Following**, **Discover**, and **Create** tabs.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    Visit tiktok.com, click **Log in** in the top right. Use the same credentials. Navigate via the sidebar for home feed, search, and inbox.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Interact with Content

Start engaging right away as a user or creator.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse and Like" icon="heart">
    Scroll the **For You** page. Tap the heart to like, comment icon to add feedback, or share arrow to send to friends.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create First Video" icon="plus">
    Tap the **+** button. Record or upload a clip, add effects via CapCut integration, then post with captions and hashtags like `#ByteDance`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow Creators" icon="users">
    Search for trending topics, follow accounts, and explore personalized recommendations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For creators embedding videos on websites, use this snippet:

<CodeGroup tabs="HTML,React">
```html
<iframe src="https://www.tiktok.com/embed/v2/VIDEO_ID" width="325" height="580" frameborder="0"></iframe>
```
```jsx
import TikTokEmbed from 'react-tiktok';

<TikTokEmbed videoId="VIDEO_ID" />
```
</CodeGroup>

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting Common Issues" default-open="false">
- **Login fails?** Clear cache or reset password via email.
- **No content?** Update the app and grant location permissions.
- **Upload errors?** Check file size (`<100MB`) and internet connection.
</Expandable>
