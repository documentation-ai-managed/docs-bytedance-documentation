<Expandable title="How do I recover a forgotten password?" default-open="true">

Follow these steps to reset your password securely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Login Screen" icon="smartphone">
    Launch the TikTok app or visit `tiktok.com/login`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Forgot Password" icon="key">
    Tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email or phone number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Identity" icon="check-circle">
    Check your email or SMS for a verification code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set New Password" icon="lock">
    Enter a strong password (at least 8 characters, mix of letters and numbers).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication after resetting to enhance security.
</Callout>

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What are the key content policies I must follow?">

ByteDance enforces strict rules to maintain a safe community.

- No hate speech, violence, or harassment.
- Respect intellectual property; do not use copyrighted material without permission.
- Avoid misinformation or spam.

<Callout kind="danger">
  Violations lead to content removal or account suspension. Review full policies at `platform.com/guidelines`.
</Callout>

For creators, tag sensitive content appropriately.

<Expandable title="Why can't I access the app, and how do I fix it?">

Common issues include network problems or cache buildup. Select your platform for tailored fixes.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
    Clear app cache and data:

    ````bash
    Settings > Apps > TikTok > Storage > Clear Cache
    ````

    Restart your device and check for updates in Google Play.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
    Offload the app:

    ````bash
    Settings > General > iPhone Storage > TikTok > Offload App
    ````

    Reinstall from App Store and sign in again.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web" icon="globe">
    Clear browser cache:

    ````javascript
    // In Chrome DevTools (F12)
    Application > Storage > Clear site data
    ````

    Try incognito mode or a different browser.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

If issues persist, verify your internet connection exceeds `>5Mbps`.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I delete my account permanently?">

Permanently deleting removes all data. Proceed carefully.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings" icon="settings">
    Open profile > Settings and privacy > Account > Deactivate or delete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Delete" icon="trash-2">
    Choose "Permanently delete account" and follow prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm" icon="alert-triangle">
    Enter password and submit. Account deactivates after 30 days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="alert">
  Deletion is irreversible. Download your data first via Settings > Account > Download your data.
</Callout>

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I integrate with TikTok API for developers?">

Use the TikTok Business API for analytics and content posting.

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
  ````javascript
  const response = await fetch('https://open.tiktokapis.com/v2/research/video/query/', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: {
      'Authorization': `Bearer ${accessToken}`,
      'Content-Type': 'application/json'
    },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      query: 'music:country',
      fields: ['id', 'create_time']
    })
  });
  const data = await response.json();
  console.log(data);
  ````

  ````python
  import requests

  headers = {
      'Authorization': f'Bearer {access_token}',
      'Content-Type': 'application/json'
  }
  data = {
      'query': 'music:country',
      'fields': ['id', 'create_time']
  }
  response = requests.post(
      'https://open.tiktokapis.com/v2/research/video/query/',
      headers=headers,
      json=data
  )
  print(response.json())
  ````
</CodeGroup>

Obtain `accessToken` from developer.tiktok.com.
